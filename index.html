Holliday Home & Apartments

Kaalvoet is  fully self-contained and consist of the luxury KAALVOET VILLA  with 4 en-suite bedrooms on the first floor and another 3 Luxury Apartments, Fynbos, Seashells and Ocean on the ground floor.

Kaalvoet Yzerfontein

KAALVOET HOLIDAY HOME AND APARTMENTS consist of the following:

A booking for two people includes the FYNBOS room with the self-catering living area and kitchen. Add the SEASHELLS room for another 2 people and the OCEAN room for 2 more people (additional fees apply).

THE KAALVOET VILLA, the entire first floor has four en-suite bedrooms. It can accommodate 8 - 10 people. It is tastefully decorated and extremely well equipped. The kitchen has a double SMEG stove, fridge/freezer and a scullery with a dishwasher and washing machine. The very spacious open-plan living area with a lounge and dining area is ideal for entertaining or just spending special time with family or friends. There is an indoor braai/fireplace and a deck overlooking the Ocean and green Fynbos area. See the spectacular sunsets of Yzerfontein from the deck.

KAALVOET APARTMENTS on the ground floor has 3 luxury suites, Fynbos, Seashells and Ocean. They can be booked separately or together, accommodating between 2 to 8 people. When interleading doors are opened it is one house with 3 bedrooms and 3 bathrooms.We offer DSTV, free Wi-Fi and parking on the premises and barbeque facilities. Daily cleaning services are not included but can be pre-arranged at an additional cost. Parking is available on the property.

KAALVOET VILLA

KAALVOET VILLA is the top floor of an upmarket self-catering holiday home with four en-suite bedrooms. It can accommodate 8 - 10 people. It is fully equipped kitchen, braai/fireplace, spacious lounge and dining room perfect for entertaining.  See the spectacular sunsets of Yzerfontein from the deck. Only 150 metres from the beach with a beautiful view of the ocean as well as a green belt of fynbos on the side. Wi-Fi, DSTV (full bouquet) and parking on the premises.  It is the ideal holiday home for family or friends to spend time together! 

A once-off cleaning fee of R600 applies.

FYNBOS

FYNBOS Self-catering apartment on the ground floor is a fully self-contained apartment and is spacious and tastefully furnished. It has 1 private bedroom with a queen-size bed, fully equipped kitchen and small lounge area with a fireplace. The open-plan kitchen area has an oven, stove and dining room table and chairs. A private braai area overlooking the Fynbos Green area with a stunning view of the Ocean is offered.

A once-off cleaning fee of R200 applies.

SEASHELLS

SEASHELLS Deluxe studio room is an open-plan, very spacious room facing the ocean and has two single beds that can be converted into a king-size bed. It accommodates 2 people.  It has a private bathroom with a shower as well as a spacious seating area. It does not have a kitchenette but is equipped with its own kettle, microwave, small fridge, cutlery and crockery and a patio with a table and chairs. A portable braai is supplied on request. Seashells is perfect to add to Fynbos for a family of 4 or 5 people. Interleading doors will be opened to make it one apartment. 

A once-off cleaning fee of R200 applies.

OCEAN

OCEAN Luxury Suite is an open plan, luxury room facing the ocean and has a queen-size bed. It also has a daybed. It offers a private bathroom with a shower and seating area with a small table and chairs. The kitchenette is equipped with its own kettle, microwave, frying pan, small fridge, cutlery and crockery and a patio with a table and chairs. A portable braai is supplied on request.  

A once-off cleaning fee of R200 applies.
